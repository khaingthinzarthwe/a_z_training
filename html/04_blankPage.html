Summer Strike Summer Strike (Korean: 아무것도 하고 싶지 않아) is a 2022 South Korean television series starring Kim Seol-hyun and Im Si-wan. It is based on a webtoon of the same Korean title by writer Joo Young-hyun. The series is an original drama of Genie TV, and is be available for streaming on its platform and on OTT media service Seezn.
